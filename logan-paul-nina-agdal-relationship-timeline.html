    How Logan Paul And Nina Agdal Met And Inside Their Relationship History

    While it's unclear as to precisely when Logan Paul and Nina Agdal started dating, we do know they first met in early 2022 at an event in New York City. According to The Daily Front Row, Nina Agdal claimed that she was the one who really wanted to approach the controversial YouTube personality.

    “I found out he was there and my gut was screaming that I had to say hi to him."

    Agdal, who has previously dated Leonardo DiCaprio, claimed that she convinced Logan Paul to have a drink with her and her friends that evening. And immediately, the two hit it off.

    "We started joking around. It instantly felt like I had known him for years, and here we are. It was wild!”

    While we don't know the precise date of this event, chances are it was during May 2022. This is because Nina Agdal had just been out of a relationship with game creator Ben Kaplan and she and Paul celebrated their one-year anniversary in May 2023.

Despite a few public outings where they were spotted by the paparazzi, Paul and Agdal didn't announce their relationship until December 2022. Paul did this in a post with multiple images of the pair of them. He simply captioned the images with...

    "Lucky me."

    A couple of weeks later, Nina Agdal also confirmed their relationship by posting images of her and her boyfriend. She captioned the images...

Moreover, he also avoided the scandals he had previously become accustomed to getting himself into. However, fans on Reddit, Instagram, and X, all seemed surprised at the speed at which Paul and Agdal got engaged.

    Paul filmed his engagement to the Sports Illustrated model in Lake Como, Italy, and broadcasted it on his YouTube channel. According to Distractify, the couple stayed at Hotel Passalacqua. The Daily Mail claimed that Agdal was genuinely shocked.
