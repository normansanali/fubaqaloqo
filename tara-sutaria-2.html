    Tara Sutaria is an Indian actor, dancer and singer. A Maharashtrian by birth, this girl became a household name, owing to her role in The Suite Life of Karan & Kabir (2012). Sutaria made her Bollywood debut with Karan Johar’s Student of the Year 2 (2019). Let’s have a look at Tara Sutaria’s life history and know some interesting facts about her.

    Biography/Wiki

    The actress was born on 19 November 1995 (aged 23; as in 2018) in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. She belongs to a Parsi Family.

    She is trained dancer and has received training in dance forms of classical ballet, modern dance and Latin American dances from The School Of Classical Ballet & Western Dance, Royal Academy of Dance, UK, and Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing, UK. Tara even bagged a full scholarship from the Royal Academy of Dance in London.

    Apart from being a dancer, she is also a singer. She started singing at the age of 7. Tara has sung in many national and international operas and competitions.

    She has pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Media from St. Andrew’s College of Arts, Science and Commerce.

    Physical Appearance

    The brown-eyed beauty is around 5’ 5” tall and weighs around 55 kg. She has black hairs.

    Family & Boyfriend

    A native of Mumbai, Tara is the daughter of Himanshu and Tina Sutaria. She has a twin sister, Pia Sutaria.

    Career

    Tara did her schooling from Bai Avabai Framji Petit Girls’ High School, Mumbai. She obtained her graduate degree from St. Andrew’s College of Arts, Science and Commerce, Mumbai.

    Being a singer, she has recorded and performed at various concerts in Tokyo, London, Lavasa, and Mumbai. She has even shared the stage with the famous Nepalese singer, Louis Banks and New Zealand performer, Mikey McCleary.

    Tara was first seen on the Television, in 2011 when she was 13, in the Indian Reality Show, Entertainment Ke Liye Kuch Bhi Karega; where she performed an opera and received a standing ovation. She won INR 10,000 from the show.

    Then, in 2010, Tara hosted Disney India’s show Big Bada Boom. She has also lent her voice for the films like Taare Zameen Par (2007) and Guzaarish (2010). She made her TV debut with the show The Suite Life of Karan & Kabir (2012). She has also played the lead role in Raell Padamsee’s production of the musical Grease.

    Tara Sutaria made her Bollywood debut with Karan Johar’s Student of the Year 2 (2019).
