    There’s only one game separating the Michigan Wolverines and the TCU Horned Frogs from the 2023 NCAA College Football Playoff National Championship.

    On Dec. 31, the two squads will face off against one another in this year’s Fiesta Bowl at Glendale, AZ’s State Farm Stadium, which determines whose season ends in 2022 or 2023.

    Jim Harbaugh’s No. 2 Wolverines come into the contest following a perfect 13-0 season, thanks largely to the standout play of quarterback J.J. McCarthy and wide receiver Ronnie Bell.

    Meanwhile, Sonny Dykes’ third-ranked Horned Frogs enter the semifinal sporting a 12-1 record.

    Now, with everything on the line, you can still purchase last-minute tickets to catch the contenders in the highly-anticipated matchup.

    At the time of publication, ticket prices start at $160 before fees on Vivid Seats.

    Lower-level seats can be found for as low as $294 before fees.

    All prices are subject to fluctuation.

    Kickoff is at 2 p.m. PST.

    2022 Peach Bowl tickets

    If Michigan and TCU aren’t your teams and you want to catch the top-ranked Georgia Bulldogs (13-0) take on the No. 4 Ohio State Buckeyes, you can, at Atlanta’s Mercedes-Benz Stadium on Dec. 31 at 8 p.m. EST.

    What a way to spend the end of the year!

    Tickets can be snagged for $211 before fees at the time of publication.

    2023 NCAA College Football Playoff National Championship tickets

    While we don’t know which two of the four squads will advance to the National Championship, we can tell you that the game will be a doozy.

    Whether it’s a highly-anticipated Michigan-Ohio State battle or an entirely different pairing, expect to see the best NCAA college football offers at SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, CA, on Jan. 9 at 4:30 p.m. PST.

    How to watch the Fiesta Bowl

    If you’re not going to be in the stands for this year’s Fiesta Bowl, you don’t have to worry— the game will be broadcast on ESPN.

    Not by a TV on game day?

    The Fiesta Bowl can also be live-streamed with an ESPN+ subscription.
