At present, she is seen portraying Caroline Spencer Forrester in the soap opera “The Bold and the Beautiful” (since 2012). Godfrey has been active in the entertainment industry since 2005.

    To begin with, Linsey was educated at South Fork High School in Stuart, but upon graduation she moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting which, later, became the main source of Linsey’s net worth. She debuted in the episode of the television drama series “One Tree Hill” (2005) created by Mark Schwahn. Then she was diagnosed with cancer, to be precise, Hodkin’s lymphoma. Consequently, she had to undergo treatment and made a seven-month career break.

Later, she played the role of Eliza in the feature film “The Assignment” (2010). Following this, she made appearances in episodes of the television series “The Suite Life on Deck” (2010), “Wizards of Waverly Place” (2011) and “CSI: Miami” (2011). All contributed to her net worth

Since 2012, she has had a regular role in the television soap opera “The Bold and the Beautiful” created by William J. Bell and Lee Phillip Bell. For the role of Caroline Spencer Forrester, the actress has been twice nominated for the Daytime Emmy Award.
