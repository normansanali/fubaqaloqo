    Pitch Perfect co-stars Anna Camp and Skylar Astin have tied the knot, according to People magazine. The adorable pair started dating back in 2013 after meeting on the set of the comedy acapella film, and were engaged in early 2016.

    Although the happy newlyweds have kept their wedding pictures private so far, the couple have been sharing sweet snaps in anticipation for their big day, revealing how excited they were to get hitched. In one photo, Anna has shared a photo of an incredible La Gartier custom made garter, writing: "thankyou so much @lagartier for these GORGEOUS custom wedding garters with the adorable@linksoflondon charm!!! Can't decide which one is prettier!

    Skylar and Anna have been dating since 2013

    Meanwhile, Skylar shared a snap of himself and his fiancée kissing while enjoying a karaoke night, writing: "Nailed it."

    Anna previously opened up about how they met, telling Glamour magazine: "We were friends first and just hit it off. He's just so great, and he's a Libra too. We didn't really have a scene together, but he's just such a fun guy. He's great.

    The couple were engaged in early 2016

    "He was dating somebody, and I had been through [a divorce] and he was like, 'Do you want to go on a date?' I remember thinking, Well, he's younger than me. He's five years younger. He's 26, and I'm 31. But I was like, 'You know what? OK! Let's do it, let's see.' He's literally my best friend."

    Skylar also chatted about the relationship in an interview on the Rachel Ray Show, saying: "I think honestly the key to our relationship is that we were friends first…then there's that moment when you take that risk and…it becomes what it is now which is a two year relationship."
