    Who is Mike Judge?

    Mike Judge whose birth name is Michael Craig Judge was born in 1962 in Guayaquil, Ecuador as the middle of three siblings. His mother, Margaret, was a librarian, while his father, William, was an archaeologist who was also working for a nonprofit in Ecuador. When Judge was seven, he and his family moved to Albuquerque, New Mexico, where he attended St. Pius X High School. Later, he attended the University of California, San Diego, graduating with a Bachelor of Science in physics in 1985.After college, Judge had a number of brief jobs in physics and mechanical engineering but soon grew bored of science. He moved to Silicon Valley in 1987 to join the startup video card company Parallax Graphics but quit after less than three months to become a bass player for Anson Funderburgh’s blues band. In 1989, he purchased a 16mm film camera and began creating his first animated short films at his new home in Richardson, Texas.
