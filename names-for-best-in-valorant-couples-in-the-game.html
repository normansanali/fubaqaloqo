    How to change your in-game name in Valorant ID:

    • Close the VALORANT client (if it’s already open)
    • Log in to your Riot account from your browser
    • Navigate to the Riot ID tab at the top left-hand side of the page
    • Type in your new name
    • Click save changes

    You can also change your tagline, which is found at the end of your name. It consists of a hashtag and a set of numbers or letters, similar to a clan tag in leagues. You have the option of changing your name and tagline once every 30 days using this method. It’s also worth remembering that changing your Valorant name will also change your Riot ID – this will affect other Riot games, such as League of Legends, Teamfight Tactics, and more, so be careful. Players have the freedom to change both elements of their in-game names to make it easier to add friends in Valorant and also have similar names.
