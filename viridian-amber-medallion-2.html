    One’s maximum stamina pool can be increased by 11% with the Viridian Amber Medallion, a Talisman found in Elden Ring. Upgraded versions of this talisman give the Stamina Stat increases of 13 and 15 percent, respectively. For Tarnished explorers who frequently expend a lot of stamina at once, the Viridian Amber Medallion is the perfect piece of gear. For example, the additional stamina points that this Elden Ring Medallion offers can allow high-bursting DPS players the necessary energy to execute one- or two-shot combinations that can swiftly dispatch their opponents. Additionally, combining the Viridian Amber Medallion with a tool that promotes Stamina Recovery, such as the Green Turtle Talisman, would be sufficient to meet one’s stamina requirements.

    The Weeping Peninsula in South Limgrave is where one can get the basic version of the Viridian Amber Medallion found in Elden Ring. Particularly, in Tombsward Cave, a Dungeon southeast of the Fourth Church of Marika, Tarnished pilgrims must confront Miranda the Blighted Bloom. Players are encouraged to carry Neutralizing Boluses before entering the cave because it is filled with poisonous mud.

    Within Leyndell’s outer ward, on the dirt road that runs between the Outer Wall Phantom Tree and the Outer Wall Battleground, is where you can find the Viridian Amber Medallion +1. Players will come upon a lone Elden Ring Commoner sitting by the roadside halfway along this trail. The mob will abruptly change into Margit as adventurers get close, starting an open-field boss battle.

    Finding All Viridian Amber Medallions In Elden Ring


    The second battle with Margit does not take place in an enclosed space, unlike the historic confrontation at Stormveil Castle, therefore Tarnished adventurers will be able to ride Torrent throughout the conflict. Be cautious, though, as there are numerous holes scattered over the battlefield that could get players stuck. Fans of Elden Ring will receive the Viridian Amber Medallion +1 once the Altus Plateau variation of Margit has perished.

    Tarnished pilgrims must enter Miquella’s Haligtree, the Legacy Dungeon to the north of Mountaintops of the Giants, in order to obtain the Viridian Amber Medallion +2. Finding the Haligtree Secret Medallion or using the “Stake-of-Marika skip” next to the Shack of the Lofty will access this region. Players must go with Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree, down the rooftop walkway from Haligtree Town Plaza to the boss arena. Turn around and cautiously jump into the somewhat concealed interior below after crossing the first gap, though. The Viridian Amber Medallion +2 can be found in a chest in the back chamber for Elden Ring players.
