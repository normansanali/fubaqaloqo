    Ciara is throwing it back to 1992.

    The singer celebrated her 36th birthday on Monday and shared the evening's look on Instagram. In the photos, she wears a black vintage dress by Dolce & Gabbana from the brand's 1992–93 Fashion Week collection, featuring a statement black tie over a white collared halter. The dress, originally worn by Linda Evangelista in fall 1992, was given a personalized revamp by stylist Kollin Carter. On Ciara, he paired the piece with a black hat, thigh-high tights, and a D&G logo belt, topped with an additional ode to the '90s: a vintage bolero jacket. Bringing a pop of color to the otherwise black-and-white ensemble, the singer appeared with honey-blonde hair—a departure from her usual dark brunette tones.

    "Celebrating in my Birthday Suit," she wrote in the caption.

    Ciara dressed up Monday to celebrate her birthday with her husband, Seattle Seahawks quarterback Russell Wilson, whom she married in 2016. The NFL athlete shared his own birthday tribute with a montage of photos of the singer on Instagram.

    "Perfect in every way," he wrote in the caption of the post. "God made you for me. He made you to fit perfectly in my arms. Made you to be the amazing woman and mother you are. God made you to entertain the world with your gift to sing & dance! He gifted you with the ability to Tilt the room.. when you walk in ALL the furniture slides towards you. He gifted you with the ability to smile and bring joy to ALL things! Gifted you with the ability to LOVE. Happy Birthday my Queen @Ciara Heaven sent!"

    Ciara and Wilson have two children together, Sienna and Win Wilson. Back in July, the two celebrated Win's first birthday, and Ciara posted a loving tribute on Instagram. "You are the cutest and sweetest baby boy!" the singer wrote. "Always dancing, smiling, and laughing…Always all over the place! The worlds going to have to keep up! :) I'm so excited and grateful to be celebrating you today! I love you so much."

    Sabrina Park is a Digital Fellow at HarpersBAZAAR.com where she covers news, fashion, and culture stories. When she’s not writing she loves hanging out with her cat, reading outdoors and scrolling the depths of TikTok.
