    Wiki/Biography

    Kori Sampson was born in 1995 (age 25 years; as in 2020) in Plymouth, Devon, UK. He did his schooling from Devonport High School for Boys, Plymouth, England and holds a degree in Nutrition.

    Physical Appearance

    Height (approx.): 5’ 7”

    Eye Colour: Dark Brown

    Hair Colour: Brown

    Family & Ethnicity

    Kori’s father’s name is Mark Sampson, and his mother’s name is Nikki Davis. He has a sister named Tilly.

    Kori Simpson with his Sister and Mother

    Career

    He was a trainer at his local PureGym. The experience that he gathered at PureGym helped him in becoming a personal trainer. He has modelled for brands like Luxe, Line, and Ruff Elegance.

    Kori Sampson in an Advertisement for Ruff Elegance

    He has also modelled for the designer Samuel Hogue. He made his entry in episode 6 of the show, Too Hot To Handle (2020), and was eliminated in episode 8 of the show.

    Kori Sampson in ‘Too Hot To Handle’

    Facts/ Trivia

    • After Too Hot To Handle ended, he went on a trip to Amsterdam with his friends.
    • He likes travelling and has been to the places, Thailand, Amsterdam, Rome, and Mexico
    • He has also authored an Ebook titled “My Lean Lifestyle,” which can be accessed from his website. In the ebook, he has given his fitness tips for the readers to achieve their fitness goals.
    • He has many tattoos; both of his arms and his right leg are filled with tattoos. On the right side of his chest, there are numbers written in Roman numerals, and there are some words inked on the left side of his abdomen.
