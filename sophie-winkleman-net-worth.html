    Sophie Winkleman Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

    Sophie Winkleman was born on August 5, 1980 in London, England. She is an actress and writer, known for The Chronicles of Narnia: The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe (2005), Two and a Half Men (2003) and Chasing Alice (2003). She has been married to Lord Frederick Windsor since September 12, 2009. They have two children. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
