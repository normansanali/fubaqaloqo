    Nicolette Scorsese Bio And Career Begining

    Nicolette Scorsese made her acting debut in a film called “A-Team” in 1985 where she played the role of Cindy. That was her stepping stone as she landed another role in a comedy series “Charles in Charge” in 1987. She was also featured in another film called “Perfect Victims” in 1988. Scorsese starred in other movies called “Boxing Helena” in 1993, “Girls in Prison” in 1994, “Christmas Vacation” in 1989, NYPD Blue (1993) and others.

    Her career climax was after starring in National Lampoon’s Christmas Vacation. Her performance in the movie landed her a nickname from her admirers. She was referred to as the “Christmas Vacation dream girl”. Nicolette decided to quit acting after featuring in the drama series NYPD Blue (1995-2000).
