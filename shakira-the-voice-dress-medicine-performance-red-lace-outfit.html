    Shakira, Shakira! The sexy songstress stole the show as she sizzled on stage in a little get-up that put her figure on full display -- and we loved it! Did you? When it comes to putting on a show, Shakira knows how to turn up the heat with one sexy costume after another -- and her latest look on The Voice on May 19 did not disappoint! Mere days after captivating us on stage at the Billboard Awards in Las Vegas, she was back on the competition show and looking just as sensational in a red lace dress with a thigh-grazing hemline.

    Shakira ‘The Voice’ Dress For Performance:

    Shakira wowed in her look last night, rocking a cocktail dress crafted by Australian designer Jaime Lee. Her long-sleeved frock kept her relatively covered up on top, but from the form-fitting silhouette to the severely short hemline it was undeniably sexy — and it looked amazing on her frame! As if the delicate red lace wasn’t enough, the ensemble also featured swavroski crystal embellishments that sparkled as she rocked the stage. The sexy songstress turned up the heat by wearing the mini with black knee-high boots and she wore her long blonde hair in her signature messy curls.

    Shakira performed her country duet, “Medicine,” with fellow Voice judge Blake Shelton — and she looked and sounded amazing!

    I’m going to miss seeing Shakira storm the stage on the competition show, especially because her sexy style is so spot-on — and her latest look did not disappoint.
