    Thorgan Hazard Bio

    His full name is Thorgan Ganael Francis Hazard. He is a professional footballer from Belgium who plays as a winger and or attacking midfielder  for the German Bundesliga club Borussia Dortmund and the Belgium national team.

    Hazard was born in La Louviere but was raised in Braine-le-Comte, a small town int the Wallonia region of Belgium. He grew up in a family where football is in their DNA. Both his mother and father were footballers in Belgium. Thorgan has three siblings—Eden Hazard, Kylian Hazard and Ethan Hazard.

    Eden Hazard is the famous Real Madrid Superstar who made his time at Chelsea FC. Kylian was playing for White Star Bruxelles and later joined Chelsea’s development team and now playing for Cercle Brugge. Ethan goes to the youth academy of Eden’s former club.

    Hazard was promoted to the senior team permanently ahead of the 2011/2012 season and assigned the number 22 shirt. He made his professional debut in Lens’ opening league match of the season against Reims, appearing as a second-half substitute in a 2–0 defeat.
