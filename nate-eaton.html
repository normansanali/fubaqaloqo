    Nate Eaton East Idaho News

    Eaton currently serves as a news director and senior reporter at EastIdahoNews.com. Here, he manages a staff of 5-10 people and determines the news content that will be posted on our website.

    It is also known that Nate actively provides video and print reports and regularly participates in community events. Most noteworthy, he helped launch EastIdahoNews.com. which received an Edward R. Murrow award and was placed first in the General Excellence online category.

    Prior, Eaton served as a reporter and anchor at WRIC TV8. Citing his work here, he reported live, breaking, and developing news as the senior nightside reporter. He also covered the execution of the D.C. sniper, the Virginia Tech shooting massacre, and the disappearance of Hannah Graham.

    Moreover, Nate often filled-in anchor evening and weekend newscasts. He has likewise appeared on CNN, FOX News, ABC, CBS, NBC, “Dr. Phil,” “Nancy Grace,” “The Doctors,” “The Today Show,” and “Good Morning America”.

    Nate Eaton KIDK TV3

    Nate worked as a news anchor and reporter at KIDK TV3, where he began his journalism career. Here, he anchored weekday 6 pm and 10 pm newscasts. In addition, he wrote and published stories daily for the station website.

    Citing his career, Eaton is a four-time recipient of the prestigious Edward R. Murrow award. Furthermore, he was named “Outstanding Reporter of the Year” four times by the Virginia Associated Press.

    Nate has also been nominated for an Emmy. He is known for being the only contestant in the world to be on “The Price is Right” and “Let’s Make a Deal.” He and his wife won the “Big Deal” by picking curtain No. 3 dressed as a banana and sock monkey.
