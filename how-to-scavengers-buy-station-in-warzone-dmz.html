    How To Scavengers Buy Station in Warzone DMZ?

    To advance in Call of Duty DMZ, you must fulfill several faction tasks. You must put on a disguise in order to reach the Scavenger’s purchase station for the new Tier 1 quest Dead With the Devil for the Shadow Company faction. Because you must visit the Scavenger’s purchase station while it is equipped and know where to obtain a Disguise field upgrade, this assignment can be challenging. Here is how to complete the Deal With the Devil assignment in DMZ, which I completed on Al Mazrah. To learn more, keep reading the article.

    Scavengers Buy Station DMZ

    You can disguise your operator as an AI warrior using the new Disguise field improvement. You can avoid detection from opposing operators in this method. On Al Mazrah and Vondel, I discovered many upgrades to the Disguise field, particularly in the hospital structures and ambulances. I’d advise keeping the field upgrade in your backpack once you’ve discovered multiple copies so you may utilize it later. Additionally, avoid firing at the opposing AI units whose appearance you have assumed. They will become hostile as a result, and your field upgrade will be useless. The first phase of the operation will be finished by equipping the Disguise.

    The Disguise should be saved, though, until you locate Scavenger on the map. Deal With the Devil’s second objective demands you to enter the Scavenger’s buy station, and you can only accomplish this when you’re disguised. When you go to the Scavenger while wearing the disguise, the boss will lead you to a nearby buy station. To finish the job, you must use that exact purchasing station. After you visit the Scavenger boss area while wearing the Disguise field upgrade, the Scavenger’s Buy Station will be indicated on the map. It is a good idea to finish a few Hunt contracts in the interim because the Scavenger boss needs to spawn on the map first.

    After taking down an opposing player, make sure to leave the area because the remaining operators will draw the Scavenger boss. To see how the Scavenger appears on the map, look at the red circle in the image above. When you come back, the Scavenger will still be there. A deployable buy station will also be indicated on the map. To finish the job, go to the purchasing station and buy the necessary supplies. Remember that you need Disguise equipped in order to visit this purchase station.
