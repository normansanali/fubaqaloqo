    Hulk Hogan is a famous American retired professional wrestler, television personality, producer, actor, business, and musician.

    Terry Eugene Bollea, better known by his ring name Hulk Hogan was born on August 11, 1953, in Augusta, Georgia, the USA to parents Pietro Peter Bollea (a construction foreman) and Ruth V. Moody (a dance teacher).

    Hulk Hogan and his family moved to Port Tampa, Florida, when he was one and a half years old. Hulk Hogan was a pitcher in Little League Baseball during his childhood, however, an injury ended his baseball career.

    With regards to his educational background, Hulk Hogan attended Hillsborough Community College. He later proceeded to the University of South Florida from where he dropped out to focus on music.

    Hulk Hogan rose to prominence as a wrestler and won his first wrestling championship after defeating Bob Roop for the ‘NWA Southeastern Heavyweight Championship’ in 1979.

    Hulk Hogan became a 12-time world champion; six times with the WWE and another six with the WCW (World Championship Wrestling). He was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2005.

    Hulk Hogan Height

    Hulk Hogan stands tall at a height of 6 feet 4 inches (1.94 m).

    Hulk Hogan Net Worth

    Hulk Hogan has a net worth estimated to be $25 million.

    Hulk Hogan Wife

    Hulk Hogan is currently married to his longtime partner, Jennifer McDaniel. The couple got engaged in November 2009 and later got married on December 14, 2010, in Clearwater, Florida.

    Hulk Hogan Children

    Hulk Hogan is the father of two children. He has a daughter named Brooke Hogan (born May 5, 1988) and a son Nick Hogan (born July 27, 1990) with his former spouse, Linda Claridge.
