    365 Days Actress Magdalena Lamparska Bio | Wiki

    Magdalena Lamparska (born on January 6, 1988) is a Polish actress who is famous for her performances in productions like “Tolko Ne Seychas,” “Tips for Cheating,” “The Zookeeper’s Wife,” “How I Fell in Love with a Gangster,” and “Banksters.”

    In 2020, Magdalena became part of a hit film named “365 Days” when she got to play the role of a character named Olga. It is a hit erotica that featured famous artists like handsome Italian hunk Michele Morrone and Polish beauty Anna Maria Sieklucka.

    Continuing the story, the makers of “365 Days” also released the second installment by the name “365 Days: This Day,” which came on April 27. Magdalena is still part of the production, and people praised her for her performance in the movie.

    On the one hand, where Magdalena is earning fame for her acting “365 Days,” people also loved her performance as the lead actress in a rom-com film called “The Taming of the Shrewd.”

     

    Personal Life, Husband, and Son

    The beauty of Polish origin is happily married to a traveling blogger named Bartek Osumek, who is also very popular on Instagram. In 2017 they got blessed with a baby boy who they named Tymoteusz. The couple is protective of their child, so they have not revealed his face until now.

    The family maintains a separate Instagram account named “@havingfam.” Here they post images, sharing moments from their personal life. So if you are interested in knowing more about this lovely family, we will suggest you follow this account.

     

    Childhood, Parents, and Education

    On January 6, 1988, Magdalena Lamparska (age 34 years) was born to her parents in Słupsk, Poland. When she was a kid, her mother put her in a choir, which is how she got into singing. Later her interest shifted towards acting from singing, and she began training at a local acting school. Lamparska’s father always supported her in her dream of becoming an actress.

    In 2006, Magdalena completed her schooling, after which she joined Aleksander Zelwerowicz State Theatre Academy. Here she studied the art more closely and learned different acting techniques like Improv, Meisner, Stella Adler, and Uta Hagen’s realism technique.

     

    Career

    The journey of Lamparska’s journey as an artist started as a chorister at Fantazja Choir in Słupsk. After graduating from Theater Academy Aleksander Zelwerowicz, the first acting role that she received was for a 2007 TV movie named “Jutro Idziemy do Kina.” It was a Michal Kwiecinski-directed romantic drama in which her role was Malgosia.

    Magdalena’s next acting job was for a TV series “39 I pól, ” where she portrayed a character called Marta. Some of the best shows that she worked in were released from 2009 to 2019 like “Na dobre i na zle,” “Hotel 52,” “O mnie sie nie martw,” “Za marzenia,” “Ojciec Mateusz,” and “39 i pól tygodnia.”

    Other than shows, Magdalena also has a significant career in the film industry which began in 2009 when she performed a minor role in Pawel Borowski’s film called “Zero.” After the first movie, she worked in more than 15 movies consisting of “The End,” “Banksters,” “Alice and the Frog,” “The Zookeeper’s Wife,” “No Panic, With a Hint of Hysteria,” and “Wkreceni 2.”
