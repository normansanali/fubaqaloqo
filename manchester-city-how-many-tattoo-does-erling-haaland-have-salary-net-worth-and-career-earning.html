    Erling Haaland’s Career Earning And Achievement

    Erling Haaland is one of the most exciting forward at his age that has emerged at the top of the list for many European football clubs.

    The striker currently plays for Manchester City, and as of 2022, he earns £18 million ($21.5 million) a year; he has five years of contracts with Manchester City.

    Before he joined Manchester city, he used to play for German Club Borussia Dortmund.

    At Dortmund, it was reported that the player had signed a contract for four and half years, and his yearly wages were £6.8 million ($9 million).

    He is an elite forward who scores goals for fun and has already achieved so many records in his short-span career.

    He has so far played for three clubs in his career. He has played for Molde club in Norway, Red Bull Salzburg in Austria, Borussia Dortmund in Germany, and Manchester City in England.

    He excelled for Salzburg in the UEFA Champions League while playing for them.

    At the FIFA Under-20 World Cup in 2019, he grabbed attention abroad by tallying nine goals for Norway U-20. They won the game by 12-0 over Honduras.

    With RB Salzburg, Haaland won the Austrian Bundesliga in 2018–19 and 2019–20.

    He also won the Austrian Cup in 2019. In 2021, he won the DFB Pokal in Dortmund.

    He won the Golden Boot at the 2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup, scoring 9 goals in a single match.

    Being the second teenager to score 10 goals in a Champions League in 2020 earned him the Golden Boy Award.

    In the same season, he also won the UEFA Champions league-best forward award.

    He also won the Bundesliga player of the season awards that were voted by fans in 2021.

    He has already been selected for the Fifa Pro World XI team as a forward in 2021.
