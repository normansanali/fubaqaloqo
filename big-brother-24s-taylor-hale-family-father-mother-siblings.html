    Big Brother 24 winner Taylor Hale‘s family consists of her father, mother, and siblings, and she has made them very proud of her as she always does.

    But that’s not all for the 27-year-old personal stylist: she was also awarded the title of America’s Favorite Houseguest, a fan-voted award, which comes with an additional $50,000 cash prize. Hale is the first “Big Brother” winner to also win America’s Favorite Houseguest prize.

    Learn more about her family here in the article below.

    Meet Big Brother 24’s Taylor Hale Family Members

    The journey of Big Brother 24 wasn’t quite pleasing for Taylor Hale, a native of Detroit, Michigan. You know she was hated by many of her fellow contestants at the start of the show and she even broke down in tears. But after all the trials and tribulation, she saw it through to the end and won (8-1 vote), the sweet revenge of success.

    Speaking with Parade, Taylor shared that she had never expected to win. “Monte has very, very strong connections with this jury. It looked like it was his jury up and down,” she said. Even though she won the game with eight of the nine jurors voting in her favor, she wasn’t the only one who was taken aback by the results.

    “Indy says, ‘I’m voting for my favorite person.’ And I literally pat Monte on the back,” Taylor said.

    In her post-game interview, Taylor acknowledged that Monte played “a very true, honest game,” and she had predicted that his performance would be “respected more.” Although many people praised Monte’s strategy, Taylor ultimately won because of her personal story and closing remarks to the jury.

    Taylor added that “Perseverance and resiliency is also a way to win this game.” Not only did she use this strategy to win the game, but she also used it to win America’s Favorite Player (AFP), which increased her $750,000 grand prize by an additional $50,000, making her the highest-paid Big Brother winner of all time.

    Currently over the moon besides Taylor is her family members. Learn about her father, mother, and siblings in this article below.

    Who Is Big Brother 24’s Taylor Hale Father, Lawrence H Hale II?

    Big Brother 24’s winner Taylor Hale’s father is Lawrence H Hale II.

    • Lawrence H Hale II Age

    Her father was born in 1958 so in January 2022, Lawrence turned 64 years old.

    • Lawrence H Hale II Job

    Talking about Lawrence’s career, he is the vice president of Reagan Mechanical in Detroit, Michigan. He is a journeyman pipefitter with more than 40 years of industry experience, he holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Construction Engineering from Lawrence Technological University.

    Furthermore, his Regan Mechanical bio states that he is a member of Pipe Fitters Union Local 636 U.A. and is the second recipient of the GARDE (Gender and Race Diversification Excellence) Award (1997). This prestigious award is given to the person most responsible for recruiting women and minorities into the plumbing and heating industry.

    A member of the State of Michigan Boiler Board and Mechanical Board, Lawrence has also received numerous Proclamations from the Michigan Governor’s Office, Wayne County, the Detroit Mayor’s Office, and the Detroit City Council.

    Currently, Lawrence oversees all construction operations for Reagan Mechanical and has the responsibility for providing field leadership, and maintaining quality and cost control of all projects.

    Who Is Big Brother 24’s Taylor Hale Mother, Jeanette Dickens Hale?

    If you look at Jeanette Dickens Hale, then you’ll see where Taylor Hale got her good looks from.

    • Jeanette Dickens Hale Age

    On 25 February 2022, Jeanette celebrated her 62nd birthday.

    • Jeanette Dickens Hale Job

    Since 2004, Jeanette has been running Trilingual Translation LLC as its proprietor. In July 2004 she incorporated her business. Since April 2022, she has worked independently as a Senior All Source Intelligence Analyst.

During her time at Marygrove College, she majored in Human Ecology and minored in French, Language Interpretation, and Translation. 1984 saw her graduate.
