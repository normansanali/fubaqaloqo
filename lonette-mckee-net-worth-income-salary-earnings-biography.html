    Lonette McKee (born July 22, 1954 in Detroit, Michigan) is an American actor, music composer/producer/songwriter, screenwriter, and director who has worked in cinema, television, and theatre. She is well remembered for her performance in the 1976 film Sparkle as Sister Williams. She has also appeared in films such as The Cotton Club, ATL, and Honey.
