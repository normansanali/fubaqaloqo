    Professional Life & Career

    Richard Madden began acting when he was a teenager. In 2011, he starred as Ashley Greenwich in the television series “Sirens” and then as Elliot in the short film “Strays.” Madden’s breakout role as Robb Stark in one of the most popular television series in the world, “Game of Thrones,” came the same year.
