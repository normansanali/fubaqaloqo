    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Jade Cargill now knows who she will defend the AEW TBS Championship against on the August 6 episode of .

    On the August 6 episode of AEW Rampage, Madison Rayne defeated “Interim Baddie” Leila Grey in her in-ring debut for the company after Cargill confronted her on the August 4 episode of AEW Dark: Elevation. After the bell, Cargill admitted that she should have handled business herself, so she challenged Rayne to step up and face her on Wednesday. Later in the broadcast, the match was confirmed for the show.

    Undefeated TBS Champion Jade Cargill invites Madison Rayne to her Open Challenge for the TBS title this Wednesday LIVE at #AEWDynamite #QuakeByTheLake on TBS!

    We are LIVE on TNT with #AEWRampage right now! pic.twitter.com/OZF2M0sEKL

    — All Elite Wrestling (@AEW) August 6, 2022

    Rayne has a noteworthy background, as she is a former five-time Knockouts Champion with TNA/IMPACT Wrestling. She also won the Knockouts Tag Team Championship three times throughout her run with the company. Meanwhile, Cargill is undefeated in singles action, and she has held the title since she won the finals of the tournament to crown the inaugural champion on the January 5 episode of AEW Dynamite.

    The updated card is as follows:

    • : Jon Moxley (c) vs. Chris Jericho
    • : Jade Cargill (c) vs. Madison Rayne
    • : Brody King vs. Darby Allin
    • : Lucha Bros vs. Andrade & Rush

    WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
