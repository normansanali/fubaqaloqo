    Who is Telli Swift? Deontay Wilder Wife/Girlfriend

    Telli Swift is a multi-talented television personality who is currently engaged to boxing champion Deontay Wilder. They are yet to tie the knot so she is not his wife at the time of publication.

    Swift, whose real name is Shuntel Swift, was into arts and entertainment since childhood. She used to participate in photo shoots and beauty pageants since she was three years old.

    Before stepping into the television world, she worked as an ER nurse. She also served as a nurse for plastic surgery recovery in Beverly Hills.

    She had her breakthrough as an actress following her stint on the popular reality series WAGs Atlanta. She starred alongside her fiance Deontay, as per Sportskeeda.

    She switched to her stage name Telli Swift after rising to fame. Moreover, she is an entrepreneur and philanthropist. She has her own perfume line.

    She also established a charitable organization called The Boxing WAGs Association.

    How Old Is Telli Swift? Age & Ethnicity Explored

    Telli Swift, age 34, was born on January 24, 1987, in Zambales Philippines.

    Yes, she is of Filipino ethnicity. She was born to her parents Reginald Swift and Vivian Crawford.

    The couple was engaged by 2018. They have one daughter named Kaori Lee Wilder together.

    Swift has another son, Kerron, from her previous marriage. Likewise, Wilder has seven other children from his past relationships.

    He had his first child, a daughter, with his ex-girlfriend in 2005. He has three children, two daughters, and one son, with former partner Jessica Scales-Wilder.
