    Maudie Bourke Obituary – Death: Co-Founder of Tipperary Drama Festival, Maudie Bourke Passes Away

    Maudie Bourke Obituary: It is with heavy hearts that we announce the unexpected passing of Maudie Bourke (nee Hayes) Garryvanus, Ballycahill, Thurles, on the 15th of December 2023, at her daughter’s residence in Templemore. Maudie, a founding member of our community, leaves behind a legacy that will forever be cherished. Predeceased by her loving husband Willie, infant daughter Bridget, brother Seamus, and sister Mary, Maudie’s departure marks the end of an era for her family and the community she held dear.

    Her memory will be a beacon of strength for those who knew her. Her devoted daughter Margaret, son-in-law Michael, and grandchildren Anna and Bill, who meant the world to her, survive her. Maudie’s sisters Betty and Joan, along with sisters-in-law, brothers-in-law, nieces, nephews, and a wide circle of dear friends and neighbors, mourn her loss deeply.

    The community will have an opportunity to pay their respects as Maudie’s body rests in Grey’s Funeral Home, Templemore, this Sunday evening from 6:30 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. A requiem mass will be held on Monday at 11:30 am in St. Cataldus’s Church, Ballycahill, where family, friends, and well-wishers will gather to bid farewell and celebrate her life.
