    Tai tuivasa Ethnicity And Nationality- Where is The MMA Fighter From?

    Tuivasa was born on March 16th, 1993, in Sydney, Australia. He holds Australian nationality. As of 2023, he is 29 years old.

    The heavyweight competitor’s mother is Kerry Johnson, an Indigenous Australian (Wiradjuri), and father is Tony Tuivasa, a Samoan. So, he has a mixed ethnic background.

    Tuivasa was raised as a Samoan, but his mother helped him discover his aboriginal roots when he was still a little child.

    The Ultimate Championship winner has several tattoos related to his ethnic background. The most notable tattoo is the one named “FTA,” which is an acronym for “From The Area.”

    He grew up in the western Sydney neighborhood of Mount Druitt. He has 11 siblings, four are females, and seven are males. Some of Anthony’s brothers are also mixed martial artists.
