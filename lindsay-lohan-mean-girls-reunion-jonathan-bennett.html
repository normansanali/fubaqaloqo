    Lindsay Lohan Just Had a 'Mean Girls Reunion' With Aaron Samuels (Jonathan Bennett) on MTV

    09 Jul 2024, 00:00

    This reunion is going to be so fetch.

    Just four days into the New Year, Lindsay Lohan is already giving us some major nostalgia thanks to a reunion with Jonathan Bennett, who played "regulation hottie" Aaron Samuels in the iconic mid-aughts film Mean Girls.

    MTV, home to LiLo's upcoming reality television show, Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club, brought the pair together again for a special Q&A surrounding the new show. For the reunion, Bennett hosted an interview between Lohan and her fans to discuss her new life in Greece and journey to building her own hospitality brand.

    Courtesy

    Lindsay Lohan and Jonathan Bennet behind-the-scenes of the MTV program Lindsay Lohan’s Beach Club: The After Show.

    The 30-minute special, which is slated to air directly after the series premiere of Lindsay Lohan's Beach Club, documents the actress' adventures in Mykonos and introduces a cast of characters who work in her infamous nightclub.

    Although MTV graced us with a selfie of Cady Heron and Aaron Samuels' reunion, they have yet to release a video sneak-peak of the duo's interview. For now, the network did unveil the first trailer for Lindsay Lohan's Beach Club. The very 2000s-esque show, which is slated to premiere on Tuesday, January 8th, documents the drama that goes down between staff at the Lohan Beach House club.
